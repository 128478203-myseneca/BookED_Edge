About

About Us

BookED is a open plataform for students and teacher that are willing to sell their used book to others in need

The process is simple, all you have to do is create an account and post all the books that you will to sell.

  • Create your account at booked.com/register
  • Select the books that you wish to sell
  • Make yourself and others happy

Really ! That is all you have to do :)
