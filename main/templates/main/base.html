About

About Us

BookED is a open platform for students and teacher that are willing to sell their used book to others in need

The process is simple, all you have to do is create an account and post all the books that you will to sell.

  • Create your account at booked.com/register
  • Select the books that you wish to sell
  • Make yourself and others happy

FAQs

FAQs

Here you can find the most frequent questions, if you have any more questions please feel free to contact us. here

What is BookED ?

BookED is a platform for students to sell and buy used books that they don't need anymore.

How do I use the book filter ?

Simply create a account, select the book filters that you wish to use in your profile and go to "Your Book Selection"

How do I buy a book ?

After creating an account go to the book page in which you wish to but a click on "Buy Book" then an email will be send to the author of the post with your information contact.

What if the Seller doesn't respond me ?

After a week if the seller doesn't respond, open up a ticket by using the "Report User" in the post that you have interest feature and describe what happened.

I have some suggestions where can I leave them ?

We are open for any kind of suggestions regarding BookED please if you have any access send it using the Suggestion Form.

What if I don't want to use the filter ?

Simply access the Marketplace which displays all books posted.
